## About CUStats

CUStats helps you monitor your AI API usage across multiple platforms:

### CUStats (macOS)
A native menubar app that shows your Claude AI and OpenAI Codex usage in real-time:

- **Real-time Usage Tracking** - Monitor 5-hour, 7-day, and model-specific limits for Claude and Codex
- **Live Countdown Timer** - See exactly when your limits reset
- **Historical Views** - Track usage patterns by day, week, or month
- **Multi-Account Support** - Manage multiple accounts
- **Native macOS App** - Built with SwiftUI, lightweight and private

### CUStats GO (iOS & Android)
A mobile companion app with home screen widgets, smart notifications, and secure credential storage:

- **iOS 16+** - Available on the App Store
- **Android** - Coming soon to Google Play
- **Widgets & Notifications** - Stay updated on the go
- **Secure Storage** - AES-GCM encryption for credentials

## How to Report Issues

You can track the plan and the progress of the development [here](https://github.com/users/luongnv89/projects/2)

### Bug Report
Found a bug? [Create a bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) with:
- Your app and macOS version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- What you expected vs what happened

### Feature Request
Have an idea? [Request a feature](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.yml) with:
- The problem you're trying to solve
- Your proposed solution

### General Feedback
Want to share thoughts? [Submit feedback](../../issues/new?template=feedback.yml)

## Before Submitting

1. **Search existing issues** to avoid duplicates
2. **Use the appropriate template** for your submission
3. **Be specific** - include version numbers and steps to reproduce
